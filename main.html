Paris, France

Paris, the City of Light, is a top travel destination known for its romantic charm, world-class art, and rich history. From exquisite cuisine to iconic landmarks, Paris is a place full of unforgettable experiences.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The symbol of Paris and a must-see landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to thousands of masterpieces including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – Iconic Gothic architecture in the heart of the city.
  4. Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue with shops, cafes, and historic landmarks.
  5. Seine River Cruise – Beautiful way to view the city, especially at night.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Buttery, flaky pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate sweets with rich flavors.
  • Baguette – Freshly baked French bread with a crisp crust.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Escargots – Traditional French delicacy served with garlic butter.
