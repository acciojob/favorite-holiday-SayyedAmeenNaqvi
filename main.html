Paris, France


Paris, the City of Light, is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world. It is known for its rich history, romantic atmosphere, art, fashion, and world-famous landmarks.

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to masterpieces like the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a stunning example of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – a beautiful avenue for shopping and dining.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – offering breathtaking views of the city.

Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissants – buttery, flaky, and freshly baked.
  • Crêpes – delicious thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Macarons – colorful and delicate French cookies.
  • French Cheese – especially Brie and Camembert.
  • Escargot – a unique French delicacy made with snails.
